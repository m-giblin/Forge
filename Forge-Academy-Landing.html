FAQ

Good to know

Is it really free?

Yes. The full Forge Academy course library and the Forge Certified PM badge are free. An optional paid, instructor-led cohort is available for teams that want live guidance.

Is this general project-management training, or Forge-specific?

Both — on purpose. You learn real PM skills (planning, sprints, capacity, reporting) and practice them live in Forge, so the concepts stick and you finish job-ready.

Do I need experience to start?

No. Module 0 takes you from zero to a working workspace. Each module builds on the last, so beginners and switchers from other tools are both covered.

How long does it take?

About three hours total across nine bite-sized modules. Go at your own pace; your progress and badge are saved.

Can my whole team get certified?

Yes. Teams can enroll together, track progress, and book an instructor-led cohort. Ask about rolling Forge Academy into your onboarding.
